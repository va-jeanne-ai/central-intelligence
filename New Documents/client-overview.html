Overview

What is Central Intelligence?

Central Intelligence replaces your separate custom AI tools with one unified platform. A central Central Intelligence (CEO) delegates to three Department Directors — Marketing, Sales, and Fulfillment — who each manage teams of Specialists and Operators. ~25 AI workers in total, all accessible through a beautiful web app with dashboards, charts, and AI chat.

Instead of switching between tools, tabs, and spreadsheets to get a picture of your business, you simply ask Central Intelligence. It knows everything happening across your organization and surfaces exactly what you need to make better decisions, faster.

Think of it as your always-on AI executive team — a CEO with three department heads who have each read every sales call, coached every client session, and tracked every marketing metric, all at once.
